About Us

Substation

Design Expertise

We provide exceptional HV design solutions that are both efficient and cost-effective.

Our HV Layout division excels in transforming Single Line Diagrams (SLDs) and engineering briefs into comprehensive layout designs. With over 13 years of experience, our team has successfully handled voltage levels ranging from 11 to 400kV for both domestic and international projects. We specialize in designing AIS (Air Insulated Switchgear) and GIS (Gas Insulated Switchgear) substation layouts, adhering to NGTSs, British Standards, and DNO standards. Our designs emphasize electrical and safety clearances, ensuring that systems are safe and maintainable.

Trust Hamza Engineering for all your HV design and layout requirements, including:
Primary Design
Civil Design
Structural Design
Cable Route and Trench Profile Drawings
